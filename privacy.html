Privacy Policy

At lens & Layered Designs, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you interact with our website, services, or content.

1. What We Collect

We may collect:

  • Your name, email, phone number, and social handles (if submitted)
  • Basic technical info like IP address, device type, browser, and site interactions

2. How We Use It

  • To respond to questions or requests

  • To comply with legal obligations

We never sell or share your personal info with third parties. Period.

3. Keeping Your Info Safe

We use encrypted communication, secure servers, and limited access protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

4. Third-Party Services

Some features like Instagram embeds or contact forms may use third-party tools that follow their own privacy rules. We only partner with trusted, secure services.

5. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to help remember your preferences and track basic engagement. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Your Rights

You can request to view, edit, or delete your personal data anytime. Just reach out via our Contact Page.

7. Updates

This policy may change as we grow. When we update it, we’ll revise the date below. Your continued use of our site means you’re cool with the changes.

8. Reach Out

Questions or concerns? Hit us up through the Contact Page or DM us at @lenslayeredDesigns on Instagram.
